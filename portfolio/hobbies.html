Hobbies

Hobbies are activities or interests pursued outside of one's regular occupation or duties, often done for pleasure, relaxation, or personal fulfillment. They are activities that individuals engage in during their leisure time for enjoyment and to explore their passions and interests. Hobbies can range from various pursuits such as sports, arts and crafts, reading, gaming, gardening, collecting items, music, cooking, or any other activity that brings joy and satisfaction to an individual. They provide a way to unwind, learn new skills, and foster creativity while offering a break from the demands of daily life.
